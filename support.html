Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first resume?

Open the app and tap on "Resume" in the main menu. Then tap the "+" button to create a new resume. You can either start from scratch or use our AI-powered resume generator to help you create a professional resume.

How does the AI resume generation work?

Our AI analyzes your job description and experience to create a tailored resume that highlights your most relevant skills and achievements. Simply provide a job description and your experience, and our AI will generate a professional resume for you.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

The Pro subscription includes unlimited resumes, unlimited cover letters, unlimited job entries, advanced templates, AI generation (50/month), PDF export, and priority support.

How do I export my resume as PDF?

With a Pro subscription, you can export your resume as PDF by tapping the share button in the resume view. This feature is available for all Pro and Pro+ subscribers.

Can I use the app without an account?

You need to create an account to save your resumes and access all features. You can sign up using your email or Apple ID for a seamless experience.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time in your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
